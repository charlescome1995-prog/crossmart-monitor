Opening hook to write:

- Avoid sounding like a product ad in the first third.
- Use CrossMart as the practical example, not the whole story.

## Monitoring is not the same as diagnosis

- A monitor tells you what changed.
- An ops dashboard should help explain why it changed.

## Signals worth combining

- Traffic keywords.
- Ad group counts and campaign scale.
- Review analysis.
- Ranking and price movement.

## Building the diagnosis layer

- Collect competitor signals.
- Normalize into JSON.
- Use AI to turn scattered metrics into operations notes.

## What ad insights revealed

- Some pages expose summary text rather than normal tables.
- Regex against the real page text can be enough for early tools.

## Hard lesson

- The useful data is often not in the DOM structure you expected.

## What I would improve next

- Add confidence levels.
- Separate observation from recommendation.
- Track changes in ad intensity over time.
