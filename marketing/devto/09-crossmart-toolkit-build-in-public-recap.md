Opening hook to write:

- Start with a concrete operator/developer pain point.
- Avoid sounding like a product ad in the first third.
- Use CrossMart as the practical example, not the whole story.

## Where it started

- A stale Amazon competitor spreadsheet.
- Manual checks that were too slow to trust.

## What got built

- Monitor for changes.
- Selector for product opportunities.
- Listing Builder for AI-assisted copy.
- Ops for competitor diagnosis.
- Simulator as the next decision layer.

## Patterns that repeated

- Snapshot-first data.
- Static dashboards.
- Real browser automation.
- AI used after structured data, not before.

## What I would do differently

- Define schemas earlier.
- Document link structure earlier.
- Separate internal and public data from day one.

## Roadmap

- More alerting.
- Better scoring transparency.
- Cleaner public demos.
- More category-specific templates.

- Ask readers what Amazon/e-commerce workflow they would automate next.
